RightRides is a multi-stakeholder cooperative

  • Passengers are global
  • Drivers are local

Revenue Sharing

  • All Drivers participate in revenue sharing
  • Any member may purchase dividend shares

Voting

  • Voting is weighted equally between driving and non-driving members

Ownership

  • All members are owners
